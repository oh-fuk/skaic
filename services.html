Operational transformation

We redesign the service flow before recommending the tool.

SkaiConnect studies where calls go, how chats become tickets, what agents see in the CRM, how managers forecast demand, and where telecom or vendor dependencies slow everything down. Then we design a cloud plan that is practical to launch and easier to operate.

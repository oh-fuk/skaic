Privacy Policy

How SkaiConnect handles website information.

This static policy page is structured for the redesigned website and should be reviewed by legal counsel before production use.

SkaiConnect may collect contact information submitted through forms, including name, email, phone number, business interest, and message details. This information is used to respond to consultation, support, and service inquiries.

Submitted information may be used to evaluate call center needs, schedule consultations, provide support, and improve services. SkaiConnect does not sell personal information through this website.

Security

SkaiConnect messaging emphasizes enterprise-grade security, encryption, and responsible data handling. Production systems should use secure form handling, access controls, and approved retention policies.
